Guardians of the Galaxy Box Office Total

 We previously reported that Guardians of the Galaxy had made $280 million domestically, and was in line to surpass $550 million worldwide in the near future.

Guardians, which features WWE Superstar Batista, has now surpassed that mark, with its new total reaching $554,304,119 million at the last count. The box office smash is scheduled to be released on DVD and Blu Ray in November.

Rosa Mendes Interview

Rosa Mendes recently talked to That Wrestling Show on SportsTown Chicago, and she talked about a number of topics, including returning to WWE after an 18 month absence, Total Divas, and more.

Mendes says she is honest and doesn’t hold back, and she likes who she is and the fans will see some fireworks this season on Total Divas.  She also talks about the situation between the Bella Twins right now, and plays a word association game talking about the other WWE Divas.
