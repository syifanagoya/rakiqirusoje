Pam Gallant is the wife of Canadian ice hockey coach and former player Gerard Gallant.

As a player, Gerard played eleven seasons in the NHL, primarily for the Detroit Red Wings, but also including a short stint with the Tampa Bay Lightning.

Gerard is the current head coach of the New York Rangers of the National Hockey League (NHL).

Who Is Pam Gallant? Pam prefers to lead a a quite life and as a result, she hasn’t divulged much information about herself.

Gallant and his wife, Pam, are the parents of two children, Melissa and Jason.

Melissa is married to former professional hockey player Darryl Boyce.

Jason, their son played four seasons with the Summerside Western Capitals of the MJAHL and currently coaches youth hockey.

Pam’s husband Gerard previously served as the head coach for the Columbus Blue Jackets (2004–2006), Florida Panthers (2014–2016) and Vegas Golden Knights (2017–2020).
