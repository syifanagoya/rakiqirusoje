HARD Summer Finally Delivers Official 2016 Trailer And Lineup

April 18, 2024
blog

SoCal electronic music festival HARD Summer has finally revealed what all of last week’s hype was about. The massive’s organizers garnered the attention of countless music fans when they unveiled their “#RIPTHEINTERNET” theme, and today they’ve revealed that they were gearing up for the release of the festival’s official trailer and lineup.

Recommended Videos

The video begins with a tongue-in-cheek skit based around the #RIPTHEINTERNET premise which features cameos from the likes of Diplo, Ghastly and Mija – as well as HARD founder Gary Richards himself. Dirtybird mainstay Justin Martin makes perhaps the most memorable appearance in the trailer for his own off-putting depiction of what happens when a modern musician must go without the internet for an extended period of time.

As for the lineup itself, HARD Summer‘s organizers have revealed a roster diverse enough to include the likes of Major Lazer, Tchami, Claude VonStroke, Dizzee Rascal, Ice Cube and Boys Noize.
