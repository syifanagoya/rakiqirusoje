    Ivanka Trump’s £3.3 million New York condo is up for grabs – and it sure is impressive! The American businesswoman and daughter of US President Donald Trump’s two-bedroom apartment boasts an impressive midtown Manhattan address down Park Avenue, moments away from the hustle and bustle of the city centre. The condo, listed on , has a breathtaking view of the Big Apple, a large living room and dining room and includes 24-hour hotel service, a daily maid service and even a live-in resident manager. The new lucky owner of the property will also have access to a fitness centre. The apartment’s master bedroom has its own en-suite marble bath and private second bath, while the second bedroom, described as a “den”, has full wall build ins.
