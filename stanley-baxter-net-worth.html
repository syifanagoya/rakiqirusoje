Born in the heart of Glasgow, Scotland, to a father who was an insurance manager, Stanley Livingstone Baxter embarked on what would become a legendary journey in the entertainment industry from a tender age.

The Early Days

Baxter’s early forays into acting began with child roles on BBC Scotland, leveraging his natural flair for performance.

His time in the British Army’s Combined Services Entertainment unit not only honed his skills but introduced him to future luminaries of the British entertainment scene.

Returning to Glasgow, Baxter’s stage career blossomed at the Citizens’ Theatre before his talents took him to London and beyond.

Television, Radio, and Film Stardom

He became synonymous with British television comedy, creating unforgettable moments on The Stanley Baxter Show, The Stanley Baxter Picture Show, and Mr. Majeika.
